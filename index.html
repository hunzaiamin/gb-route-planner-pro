What was improved from the detailed map
  • Added more locations visible in the detailed map, including KKH towns, Kaghan/Naran villages, Hunza, Ghizer, Chitral, Astore, Deosai, and Skardu-side routes.
  • Added selectable From and To locations instead of only fixed Islamabad-to-Gilgit routes.
  • Added automatic route calculation and route highlighting on the schematic map.
  • Added segment labels showing approximate distance and time between towns/villages.
Important limitation

Distances and times are approximate planning estimates. Before travel, verify live road status, weather, police/GB tourism advice, hotel availability, and local driver guidance. Mountain routes may change due to landslides, construction, snow, and security restrictions.
